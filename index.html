Projects & Milestones

Research Dissertation University of Sussex May 2023

Conducted research and analysis of data from the UCL Millennium Cohort Study of 13,000 families to determine how parental engagement impacts children's school outcomes.We created multiple linear regression models using R to throughly analyse the data and test our hypothesis.

We found that higher levels of parental interest in their children's education have a positive impact on children's likelihood of attending university and successfully transitioning from primary to secondary school.
